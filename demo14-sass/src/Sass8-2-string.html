字符串函数

参数: $greeting:'Hello Eric'
  1. to-upper-case($greeting) = 'HELLO ERIC'
  2. to-lower-case($greeting) = 'hello eric'
  3. str-length($greeting) = 10
  4. str-index($greeting,'Hello') = 1 索引值从1开始
  5. str-index($greeting,'Eric') = 7 索引值从1开始
  6. str-insert($greeting,'.net',10) = 'Hello Eric.net'
